## FCC Broadband Label (Nutrition Label) — Ground Truth

The **FCC Broadband Label** is the official, standardized disclosure required by the Federal Communications Commission. It shows:
- **Actual speeds** at your address (not advertised maximums)
- **Data caps** or "unlimited" designation
- **Monthly price** (base rate, not promotional)
- **Equipment fees** (modem, router, installation)
- **Contract terms** (if any)
- **Early termination fees** (if applicable)
